What is Data Daddy?

We are building a next generation data brokerage network to give users back control of their data online and get them paid if they choose to share their data.

Data Daddy Mobile Data Wallet

Through the app, users can monetize their data directly with the data consumers and apply legally binding terms of use around that agreement.

Data Brokerage Service

Our brokerage service engineers the shared user profiles into data products and brings them to market for easy consumer access and purchase.

Artificial Life Authentication

Proprietary technology to enable secure self-authentication with web services, enabling all internet functionality without ever being forced into sharing sensitive personal data.

What makes Data Daddy so special?

We are building a thoughtfully crafted authentication service and data network to change the current data sharing dynamics on the internet.

Take control of your online data and customize its accessibility to third parties. Data Daddy empowers users to prioritize privacy and data security while facilitating full internet functionality.

How do we help users?

We are advocates for our users. No hidden agreements and no fine text. We are up front with our business model and our operational ethos.
